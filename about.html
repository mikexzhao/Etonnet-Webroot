About Us

DESIGN & ENGINEERING CONSULTING SERVICES

Transforming concepts into fully realized, market-ready products.

Etonnet is a premier engineering firm offering a comprehensive suite of electronic and mechanical design, engineering, and consultation services. Our seasoned consultants are industry experts dedicated to bringing professional, high-impact expertise to every client project.

For more than two decades, Etonnet has delivered top-tier engineering and manufacturing solutions. We specialize in producing highly reliable, integrated assemblies that enhance product value and drastically simplify the design-to-production process.

We proudly offer full-scale Product Development Management. Our deep engineering expertise bridges both electronic and mechanical disciplines, ensuring a seamless development journey for our customers.

Our proven process encompasses every crucial step: electronic schematic design, PCB layout and fabrication, strategic component procurement, precision PCB assembly, stringent product testing, and custom retail packaging design.
