Services

Etonnet Engineering: From Concept To Market

Etonnet Engineering delivers elite electrical and mechanical engineering services tailored for technology-driven companies. We offer complete product development management as a turnkey service. Our comprehensive process covers everything from initial electronic circuit design (schematics) and PCB layout to bare-board fabrication, component sourcing, and final PCB assembly. We also handle critical final steps, including rigorous product testing and the design of custom plastic or metal enclosures and retail packaging.

We offer the following Electronic & Mechanical Engineering services:

  • Electronic Circuit Design
  • PCB Design and Layout
  • Firmware Development
  • Software Development
  • Prototypes & Manufacturing
  • Testing
  • Certifications
  • Mechanical Design and Engineering
  • Injection Molding Tools Design
  • Cardboard box Design

PCB Design & Prototyping

Leveraging over 20 years of proven experience, we have successfully developed hundreds of custom electronic products. We pride ourselves on delivering uncompromised quality at highly competitive costs.

Turnkey Electronic Engineering

Etonnet Engineering provides rapid, dependable turnkey PCB prototyping and manufacturing. Our end-to-end services span complete electronic circuit design, sophisticated PCB layout engineering, bare-board fabrication, strategic parts procurement, and precision assembly of the finished prototype.

Partnering with us for your printed circuit board needs ensures strict cost containment, accelerated development cycles, and access to the latest, most advanced PCB design software.
